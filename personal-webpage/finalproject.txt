There will be a confirm pop up to ask if the player wants to play
If you click yes, all the things will appear
You have to click the screen for the game to start
You press down at the rectangle and drag it from left to right
When the ball hit the rectangle, it will bounce
When the ball hit the brick, the brick will disappear and the ball will bounce back
If you clear all the bricks, you win the game
If the ball hit the bottom of the canvas, you lose a life
If you lose all 3 lives, the game is over
There will be an alert to say if you win or lose at the end of the game
There will be another confirm to ask if you want to play again or not
